Processes - Speed Up Relation¶

Half seems to be not responding to the increase in processes and starts flatenning at 8 processes,
Original has better speed up but it decreases with a slower rate than Half,
Double seems to have a linear correlation between Processes and speed up

Efficiency - Processes Relation¶

Half seems to be decreasing rapidly in efficiency as number of processes increase indicating inverse relationship,
Original showcases the same signs as Half, where efficiency decreases with increasing the number of processes but with a lower degree of effect,
Double experience low decrease in efficiency by increasing in number of processes
